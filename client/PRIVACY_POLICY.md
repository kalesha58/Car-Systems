## 2. Information we collect

We collect and process the following types of data:

### 2.1 Information you provide

- **Account and profile:** Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register or sign in. Profile details you add (e.g. profile photo, preferences).
- **Vehicles:** Details of vehicles you add (make, model, year, registration, photos) for your profile and for listings if you are a dealer.
- **Addresses:** Delivery and billing addresses, including location (e.g. for delivery, dealer locations, service locations).
- **Business registration (dealers):** Business name, address, tax/registration details, bank account details for payouts, and shop photos.
- **Communications:** Messages and content you send in chat, and any content you post (e.g. social feed, reviews).
- **Orders and payments:** Order details, payment method information (processed by our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers). Transaction-related data is handled according to our payment provider’s policy.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device and app usage:** Device type, OS version, app version, and general usage (e.g. to fix crashes and improve the app). We do not sell this data.
- **Location:**  
  - **Precise location:** Used only when you use features that need it (e.g. delivery address, “use current location”, live location in chat, delivery tracking, dealer/service location). We request permission before collecting precise location.  
  - **Approximate location:** May be used for things like showing nearby dealers or services when you choose to use such features.
- **Photos and media:** Only when you explicitly choose to upload (e.g. profile photo, vehicle photos, chat images). We do not access your photo library without your action.

### 2.3 Information from third parties

- **Payment processing:** Our payment provider (e.g. Cashfree) may collect and process payment data in line with their privacy policy. We receive only what we need (e.g. transaction status, order identifiers).
- **Maps:** When you use map or location features, map providers may process location data according to their policies (e.g. Google Maps).

We do not sell your personal or sensitive data to third parties.
