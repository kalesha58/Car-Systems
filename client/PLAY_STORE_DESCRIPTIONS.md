Motonode brings cars, dealers, and buyers together in one place. Whether you’re looking for your next vehicle, want to book a test drive, or need service and parts—Motonode makes it simple.

FOR BUYERS
• Browse new and used vehicles from verified dealers
• Compare cars side by side and filter by make, model, price, and more

FOR DEALERS

• Manage service bookings and workshop tasks
• Stay on top of sales and growth with clear insights

EVERYONE
• Social feed: share and discover posts from the automotive community
• In-app chat and groups to connect with dealers and other users
• Notifications so you never miss an update or offer
• Multi-language support for a wider reach
• Privacy controls and transparent terms

Download Motonode—your one app for buying, selling, and caring for cars.
